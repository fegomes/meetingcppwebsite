KDAB – Experts in Qt, OpenGL and C++

KDAB offers experienced software experts to help deliver functional, high-performing and innovative software across embedded, mobile and desktop platforms for projects using C++, Qt, QML/Qt Quick, OpenGL, HTML5, JavaScript and REST APIs.

We offer consulting, workshops, mentoring, effective processes and tools as well as market leading training in Qt, modern C++, OpenGL and more. 

We are about 90 people, located all around the world. Our Head Office is in Sweden and we have other offices in Germany, France, the UK and the USA.
