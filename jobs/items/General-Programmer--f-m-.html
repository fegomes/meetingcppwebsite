General Programmer (f/m)

published at 18.12.2017 11:33

This position will be full-time and located at Dear Reality, Düsseldorf (Germany).
As a General Programmer you will develop systems for a wide-range of 3D audio
technologies facilitating pro audio, gaming, VR & AR productions. You will also design,
implement, and ensure the proper functioning of new software tools.

Your tasks:

  • Develop 3D Audio technology in collaboration with domain experts, technical artists and other programmers
  • Debug and fix issues in dearVR powered products as they arise
  • Re-factoring of our proprietary engine to make it ready for major releases
  • Document the work to enable artists and programmer to understand how our systems work

Your qualification:

  • 3+ years of programming experience across audio, game engine and/or tools
  • Excellent C++ programming skills
  • Good knowledge of the C# language
  • Very good knowledge of advanced mathematics (3D linear algebra…)
  • Highly developed communication skills in English
  • Knowledge how to work with Unity game engine.
  • Self-motivation, good communication skills
  • Proactive and team orientated personality
  • Familiar with pair programming

Additional Information

We offer a highly motivating challenge for team players interested in showing
personal initiative in an innovative and international company. If you are passionate
about audio and would like to join a rapidly growing company, please get in contact
with us.
Applications should include the earliest starting date and your salary expectation.
Please send your application documents at jobs@dear-reality.com
