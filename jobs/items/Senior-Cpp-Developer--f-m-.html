Senior C++ Developer (f/m)

published at 18.12.2017 11:16

This position will be full-time and located at Dear Reality, Düsseldorf (Germany).
As a Senior Software/C++ Developer you will work within the development group of
Dear Reality, shaping the world of 3D audio.

Your tasks:

  • Be part of the Dear Reality Development Group with a strong focus on optimisation of our core technology.
  • Collaborate with our team of developers.
  • Development and maintenance of the dearVR core technology.
  • Be the point of contact for all questions C++ related.

Further Responsibilities include:

  • Be the expert for PC related features
  • Challenging and improving design patterns in our core products
  • Teaching our staff, learning from them and establishing an empowering working environment

Your qualification:

  • Strong knowledge of C++
  • A minimum of 5 years of experience as Software Developer
  • Strong knowledge of established design patterns
  • Good knowledge in multithreaded real-time environments
  • Great interpersonal and communication skills; we integrate directly with a lot of off-site projects around the world
  • Good written and verbal communication skills in English
  • Independent and target-oriented working method

Plus:

  • Familiar with DSP algorithms and Audio programming
  • Familiar with C#
  • Familiar with pair programming

Additional Information

We offer a highly motivating challenge for team players interested in showing
personal initiative in an innovative and international company. If you are passionate
about audio and would like to join a rapidly growing company, please get in contact
with us.
Applications should include the earliest starting date and your salary expectation.
Please send your application documents at jobs@dear-reality.com
