Senior Software Engineer (C++) (M/F)

published at 19.01.2018 11:43

Job description

Implement efficient algorithms for parsing, mapping and managing messages

Programming and further development of the graphical user interface

Development of ideas for product improvement

Implementation of bug fixes and updates

Tests and documentation

Set-up of systems on-site at clients offices as well as specification of adaptations

 

Required Skills

University Degree (i.e. Computer Science, Software Engineering, Business Informatics)

5+ years of professional experience in software development – ideally in the financial services industry

C++, Unix and Solaris development

Understanding of Messaging patterns

Basic understanding and willingness to become proficient in Java

Creative problem-solving abilities

Experience with relational and NoSQL databases

Understanding concepts of agile methodologies

Fluent in both English and German (spoken and written)

 

What we offer

Comyno is a software and business consultancy FinTech company with well-known customers from the financial industry and a clear focus on securities finance and regulatory technologies. We are developing an innovative, highly efficient middleware that enables technical communication between financial institutions and trading platforms. This is not just about data exchange, but also about the provision and implementation of unique and complex business processes.

Above average, competitive salary with a variety of social benefits

Open-ended, full-time contracts

Flat hierarchies and the opportunity to closely work with highly talented and motivated colleagues

Interesting and varied projects, international working environment and a chance to travel

Extend your knowledge (through mentors, external trainings & workshops), become a well-regarded industry expert

The chance to work with a wide range of clients in investment banking, asset management and related financial services providers

A modern office in the heart of Frankfurt’s banking district – including a terrace with a BBQ grill and skyline views
