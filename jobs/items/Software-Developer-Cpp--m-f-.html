Software Developer C++ (m/f)

published at 22.03.2018 14:47

Location: Lingen (Ems), Germany

Company: ROSEN Technology & Research Center GmbH

Join the team!

We are passionate developers who work together, share our knowledge and help each other. Periodical code reviews and community meetings ensure that we constantly improve our skills. We take the time to refine and redesign existing code to guarantee the highest level of software engineering. We are versatile and always eager to tackle new problems as we continue to push technology forward.

We work in multi-cultural teams with strong design skills and extensive experience in growing software in a way that it can easily be improved and extended for several years. Additionally, we attach great importance to having enough scope for perpetual learning and getting better together.

Consequently, we’re always using state-of-the-art technologies and tools.

We are looking for a smart, self-motivated Software Developer C++ to join a team which

  • Designs and develops high performance software solutions for processing large quantities of inspection data
  • Uses the newest C++ compiler features as they become available
  • Maintains a modern code base which makes extensive use of STL and Boost
  • Implements specific draft standard library features to benefit from them as early as possible
  • Uses a proprietary dimensional analysis library – like Boost.Units but tailored to our needs
  • Makes exclusive use of RAII to manage resources
  • Uses meta-programming when it improves safety and avoids redundancy in the code
  • Optimizes and parallelizes algorithms using external libraries like TBB, IPP, and OpenCV
  • Ensures software quality using Google C++ Testing Framework and a proprietary integration test framework written in Python
  • Communicates confidently in English and German

ROSEN is a leading privately owned company that was established as a one-man business in 1981. Over the last 35 years, ROSEN has grown rapidly and is today a worldwide technology group that operates in more than 120 countries with over 3,000 employees.

ROSEN is an extended team of people with a passion for technology and innovation. Our claim “empowered by technology” is the key to our sophisticated and highly innovative products and services to the oil and gas and other engineering industries. The focus of all our research efforts and high tech solutions is on a safe operation of assets and therefore provide protection for both people and the environment.

We offer

  • Various development opportunities
  • The opportunity to work in an international, innovative and sustainability-oriented company
  • Scope for perpetual learning and getting better together (e.g. frequent Coding Dojos, discussions, conferences)
  • An open minded, trusting, fun, yet demanding working atmosphere
