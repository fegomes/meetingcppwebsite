Senior Software Developer C++ (m/f)

published at 02.05.2018 18:46

Location: Berlin, Germany

Company: Younicos GmbH

Your main tasks are

  • Software Development in C++, Python as member of the C++ developer team
  • Development of algorithms and technical solutions in close cooperation with engineering
  • Development, maintenance and improvement of automated integration test infrastructure
  • Support of plant commissioning of the ACBM, on site or remotely
  • Definition of Interfaces between the ACBM and internally developed components in cooperation with the respective teams
  • Definition of interfaces between the ACBM and third party components (e.g. inverters, batteries, measurement devices) in cooperation with the respective suppliers
  • Writing specification as part of the software development process

Your profile:

  • Master’s degree or equivalent in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Technology or adjacent discipline
  • Significant expertise in C++, Python
  • Experience with BDD/TDD as well as with industrial bus systems like CAN, Profibus, DeviceNet etc.
  • Intuitive understanding of software architecture and design
  • Desire for writing and testing clean, efficient and reliable code
  • Strong focus on teamwork and a commitment to always let the best idea win
  • Exceptional attention to detail, thoroughness and accuracy are necessary in every facet of this job.
  • Goal oriented approach
  • Results-oriented cooperation in development programs
  • Openness to new ideas
  • A base knowledge and understanding of the principles of electrical engineering would be helpful
  • Fluent language skills in German and English, verbal and written

What we offer

  • The opportunity to send the fossils into retirement and to let them rest in peace
  • Motivated international team
  • Freedom in the organization of working activities and a high level of responsibility

ABOUT YOUNICOS

Younicos is the pioneer and market leader in game-changing energy and grid solutions based on battery storage. We provide utilities, independent power producers, microgrids and C&I customers with turnkey storage systems, supporting a wide range of business opportunities. Younicos employs 130 storage experts in Berlin, Germany and Austin, Texas, US. In July 2017 Younicos was acquired by Aggreko and is now a wholly-owned subsidiary of the global leader in mobile power, heating and cooling.
