Software Engineer - Systems and Tools (C++)

published at 04.04.2018 10:42

Location: San Jose

Company: AutoX

AutoX is an artificial intelligence company, based in Silicon Valley, and is creating full-stack A.I. software solution (perception, planning, and control) for self-driving vehicles to achieve level 5 full autonomy.  Our disruptive innovation can deliver autonomous driving to more people, sooner, and safer.

For more information, please check our website: autox.ai

WIRED |  Business Insider |  MIT Technology Review

Responsibilities

  • Develop scalable frameworks to support many types of vehicles and configurations
  • Design efficient components and optimize existing software for limited compute platforms
  • Improve onboard software stack stability, health monitoring, and error reporting
  • Collaborate with other engineers to implement, integrate, and deploy robotic perception, mapping, localization, and sensor calibration software
  • Implement distributed software to process vehicle data and perform validation testing.

Qualifications

  • BS or a higher degree in Computer Science or equivalent
  • 3+ years of industry experience using C++ in production
  • Solid understanding and hands-on experience in one or more of: Operating Systems, Databases, Concurrency, Linux Kernel, Compilers, Distributed Systems, etc.
  • Fluency in object oriented design, design patterns, and good software practices.
  • Familiarity with VTK and Qt application framework, or similar graphics framework is a plus

What We Offer

  • Working at one of the fastest growing and most promising self-driving car startups
  • A great work environment that supports growth and development
  • Highly competitive compensation and benefits package with 401k match
  • Lunch, dinner, and fully stocked kitchen
